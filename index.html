⚠️ Important: Background Location Access Disclosure

This App collects your location data even when the App is closed or not in use.

The FHR App requests access to your device's background location (precise GPS location, including when the App is running in the background or is not actively open on your screen).

Why we collect background location data:

  • Attendance Tracking: To record and verify employee clock-in and clock-out based on physical location.
  • Field Employee Monitoring: To track field staff during active work shifts to ensure operational efficiency and safety.
  • Geo-Fencing: To detect when employees enter or leave designated work zones or job sites.
  • Task & Work Location Verification: To confirm that tasks are completed at the correct locations as required by your employer.

Background location data is collected only during active work assignments as authorized by your organization. It is not collected outside of working hours unless your organization's HR policy requires it and you have been explicitly informed.

Your Consent: By using this App and granting location permissions, you explicitly consent to the collection and use of your background location data for the HR management purposes described above. You may withdraw consent at any time by disabling location permissions in your device settings, but this may prevent certain features from functioning.
